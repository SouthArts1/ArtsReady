About ArtsReady

Plan for "what if" instead of "why me." The fire. The flood. The storm. The broken pipes. The break-in. The disaster. The unforeseen happens too often. The unexpected, once-in-a-lifetime, won't-happen-to-me, I-don't-want-to-talk-about-it-possibility, happens.

Features for Members

  • Assess the readiness of your organization
  • Prioritize actions that your organization can take to become more ready
  • Take action. Assign staff, create deadlines, store readiness information
  • Post your own stories and calls for help
